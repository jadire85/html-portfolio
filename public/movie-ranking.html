The Best Movies According to Jana

My top 3 movies of all-time

  1. The Intouchables

    It's monumental and outrageously good.

  2. Inglourious Basterds

    Energetic, inventive, swaggering fun, Quentin Tarantino's Inglourious Basterds is a consummate Hollywood entertainment--rich in fantasy and blithely amoral.

  3. The LOTR: The Fellowship of the Ring

    I see it as nearly perfect: It's one of the best fantasy pictures ever made.
